FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: Two Brands
This is a broad question that might be better served by posting it on Sax On The Web.
 
Vandoren mouthpieces are very well made factory mouthpieces.  They offers several designs for alto and tenor sax.  They have fewer offerings for soprano and bari.  They have bright and dark designs for tenor but just medium to bright designs for soprano.  IMO
 
Selmers are more inconsistent in their facing work.  They offer several designs but they all tend to be medium to bright.
