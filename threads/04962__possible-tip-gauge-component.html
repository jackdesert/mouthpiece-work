FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul C.)
SUBJECT: Re: Possible tip gauge component?
A 3" base would only give 1 1/2" out to one side, which is 37 mm.  With a typical tenor facing of 22 mm length, that gives only 37 - 22 = 15 mm to put on the table of the mpce.  For bari that would be 37 - 25 = 12 mm.  Just not enough...
   
  I don't think a 4" base is enough either.
   
  BUT this is certainly going in the right direction!  Perhaps a piece of barstock could be attached in such a way to extend over?
