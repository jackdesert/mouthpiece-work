FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul C.)
SUBJECT: Re: Chamber work. How much is too much?
I have found out the hard way, excavating a mouthpiece in an effort to make it sound bigger, be more "free blowing" etc is counterproductive.  
   
  Also, the more material that is removed, the farther the mouthpiece must be shoved onto the cork to tune AD0.  Then the mouthpiece is too short and plays sharp in the high notes.  Intonation goes whacky.
   
  Paul
