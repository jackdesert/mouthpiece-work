FROM: flemingml2000 (flemingml2000)
SUBJECT: Re: repairing chips in hard rubber mouthpiece
I'm still looking for a good material to repair a white bite plate on my Brilhart.  I looked at krazy glue (cyanoacrylate) and decided against it.  First, it isn't completely waterproof.  The MSDS for it says it is soluble in water and alcohol.  I found posts on the web where people were complaining that krazy glue failed when used on their dentures (go figure).  Second, the MSDS says it is "dangerous" if injested (and didn't say if that was after it was hardened).  Third, cyanoacrylate is reported to be related to cyanide.  That was enough for me.

Mark
