SUBJECT: Re: Theo Wanne owes me money
If any of you paid by credit card you can invoke any number of Federal Regulations with your credit card issuer to receive a FULL (and complete) refund.  It's called a chargeback - happens every day.  First you have to call your credit card issuer and give a verbal complaint. Then you will need to immediately follow-up with a written complaint on a special form which is provided by the credit card issuer.

Even if much time has passed (lets just say for this discussion - less than 6 months) you can still get a refund via the chargeback route.

If you paid other than by credit card, make sure you use one next time - and just give Theo some time.  We've done much business with Theo, mostly wholesale with a sprinkle of retail, and have been very pleased with Theo.  His heart has always been in the right place and we've always had our $$$ matters squared away.

SO - take action or be patient as the case may be & best of luck with your "mouth-peeces".
