FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul Coats)
SUBJECT: Re: Low-note problem on Lawton
What is the facing length?  If the facing is very short, or if it is not 
a smooth curve, there can be difficulty with the low notes.  I have seen 
some Levelair * facing mouthpieces with a bizarre facing contour, a 
sharp radius at the break, and from there out to the tip nearly flat.  
Changing to a smooth curve really improves these pieces.

 

Paul
