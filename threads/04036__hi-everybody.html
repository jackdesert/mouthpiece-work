FROM: vincelahorra (vince lahorra)
SUBJECT: hi everybody!
i'm vince lahorra, 38, from manila, the philippines. i took up the sax in 1990 at what i call the 
University of Self Teaching and Experience (yup, no formal training 'cept for classical piano 
lessons since i was nine). stan getz is my main influence, straight ahead jazz is my devotion. 

i've been a gigging pro for ten years now. my current gear includes a 1968 mark VI tenor and 
an S-901 yanagisawa soprano, both of which i'm very happy with.  i recently treated myself to 
a new ref 54 tenor. two words: tenor heaven! sorry guys, the mark VI ain't for sale. 

it's good to have a resource such as this group that focuses on 'pieces. informative and 
enlightening. and i was just browsing at the messages...

vince
