FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul Coats)

Sigmund:  I have several diameters of aluminum tubing
which I buy in 6 foot (just under 2 meters) lengths
from a local hardware store. The wall thickness
appears to be about 2mm.

For copper, I look at the copper water pipe nipples
and tubing in the plumbing section.  You can find even
more diamters, both the basic tubing sizes, and in the
fittings, the ends are slightly larger to slip over
the standard tubing sizes.  So, that gives you a lot
of sizes to chose from.
