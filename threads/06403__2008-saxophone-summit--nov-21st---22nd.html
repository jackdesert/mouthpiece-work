Saxophone Summit - 2008




 

An industry wide saxophone event, the first annual Saxophone Summit will
feature some of the world's top saxophone performers giving concerts and
master classes. Booth exhibits by the leading manufacturers in the industry
will exhibit hot new saxophone products. This event will be presented and
hosted by International Woodwind Inc. 

 

The world's smallest saxophone will be on display, as well as 3 different
brands of Contrabass saxophones, as well as Altos, Tenors, Baritones,
Basses, and much more!!!  We will have on display a collection of rare and
exotic saxophones. $25 admission for the weekend or $20 in advance for
students and AFM members.

 

Dates:  Friday - Saturday, November 21st - 22nd 2008. 

Location:  Local 47 Professional Musicians Union, Hollywood, CA

in the newly renovated 1st floor auditorium.

817 Vine St, Hollywood, CA 90038
