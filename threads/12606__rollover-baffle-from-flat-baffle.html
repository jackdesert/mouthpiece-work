FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: Rollover Baffle from Flat Baffle
You need to open the tip to get enough material to shape a rollover.  Then you need to close the tip if it is too open.   Using epoxy or layers of lacquer (clear fingernail polish) can work to build up a rollover baffle.  But I do not really like how it turns out.
