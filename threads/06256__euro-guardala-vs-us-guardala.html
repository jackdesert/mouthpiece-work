FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul C.)
SUBJECT: Re: Euro Guardala vs US Guardala
Guardala sold his company... twice.  This is a matter of public record.
   
  He sold the company to LA Sax in the US.  He also sold the company to a man in Germany.  Both produced mouthpieces under the Guardala name.  And his employee/business partner (Powell?) sold mouthpieces from their stock.
   
  The confusion is of Guardala's own making.
