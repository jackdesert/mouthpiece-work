work!

:-)

I'm using one the I bought at www.contenti.com (jewelry and 
metalsmithing tools) - called a Three Lens Multi-magnifier -

"These magnifiers have 3 lenses: one binocular lens, a second 
binocular lens behind the first that flips up, and a pivoting loupe. 
Using different combinations of these lenses, 4 magnifications are 
possible: 1.8X, 2.3X, 3.7X, and 4.8X."
