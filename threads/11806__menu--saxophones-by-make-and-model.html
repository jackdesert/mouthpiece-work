As part of the total renovation of www.saxgourmet.com, we're adding a section about individual horns and models…..this will include pictures, serial number information, original advertisements, and brief histories……I've been accumulating this information for YEARS……if you have ANYTHING you would like to share, I would sincerely appreciate it…..if you see any omissions from the list below, please let me know…..I actually have (varying amounts of) information on ALL of the models listed below………the goal is to make sax gourmet.com the ultimate saxophone reference and information resource site…..we will NOT be selling anything there…..just giving away information for the good of the saxophone community……I've got so much stuff I don't honestly know exactly what all I have, but if you think I've missed something or have something to share, please let me know
