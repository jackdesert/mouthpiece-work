Joe:

To answer your question: our various alto models all have different bore diameters and tapers. The Marcel is the smallest diameter with the least taper; the Super 400 is the largest with the most pronounced taper; the Voodoo Rex falls somewhere in between. The Voodoo Rex is the closest to the Buescher 400, although it is in no way a copy of the Buescher, but is our unique design. If you need more information, please email me privately.
