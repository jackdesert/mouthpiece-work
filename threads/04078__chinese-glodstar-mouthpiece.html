FROM: keith29236 (Edward McLean)
SUBJECT: Chinese Glodstar Mouthpiece
Wishing to extend my relaying experience to metal without destroying a
good 'piece, I invested in a Glodstar tenor replica of a Selmer Jazz.
This is sold on eBay U.K. for 35GBP and comes in a Selmer lookalike
box with deliberate spelling changes to overcome copyright enfringements.
Complete with ligature and cap in sLiver or gLod plate. 8# = Selmer F
I could'nt get a sound out of it until I flattened the table.
Its fairly well finished but the rails had to be narrowed and the tip
was rather square.
I put on a Link facing from the files section (thanks Keith) and I am
very pleased with the results.
I would recommend this as a cheap blank.
Has anyone tried the RED DRAGON, another larruping 'piece which will
bring long life and much happiness ?  :)
