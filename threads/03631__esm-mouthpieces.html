FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: ESM mouthpieces
The ESM web site is in the Links area.  Not that that helps.  There 
are no pics of the chamber.  I have not worked on an ESM.

Modifying the mouthpiece comes down to tools, ability, risk, and 
reward.  High speed roarty tools are nice for chamber and sidewall 
work, especially with a flex shaft and a foot switch.  But HR pieces 
can be worked on with just hand files.  Riffler files are handy for 
curved work.
