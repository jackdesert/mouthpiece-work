FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: Bright Soprano Mouthpieces
I have not played the Ponzol M1, but it may be a tad darker than the Link and Dukoff.  The Ponzol would probably play better out-of-the-box.  
 
You might want to check out the Runyon line.  They are all bright and play in tune for most players.
 
You will find more responses from posting your question on the Sax On The Web forum.  There are many more members there who may have tried all three mouthpieces.
