SUBJECT: Re: Buffet (?) mouthpieces) - what are they
The stock Buffet pieces are acrylic, made by ESM in Germany. The 125 
is the tip opening - 1.25mm, or about .048".

They reface fairly well - I do them as student mouthpieces with 
a .045 tip and a ML facing. I'll post my numbers for them from work, 
if you're interested.
