FROM: stencilmann (stencilmann)
SUBJECT: Dukoff Personal Model with Super Power Plus chamber
Has anybody seen one of these? They sell for $60 more than the 
other "standard" chambers (M,D,P,S,X,L,H). No mention of them on the 
Dukoff web site which hasn't been updated in ages. One could only 
hope that the extra cost is due to quality hand finishing. Ya' never 
know.

The info on the WWBW web site: "Designed to provide the player with 
better projection and volume, the Super Power Plus offers a powerful 
tone with good control and an even scale."  Are they saying that the 
standard chambers don't offer any of those "features"? Funny... and a 
bit tragic.

Its just another reason for this love/hate thing I have for Dukoff 
mouthpieces.
