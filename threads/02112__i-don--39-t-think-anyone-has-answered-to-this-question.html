FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Kits
If you have not seen it yet, take a look at the Files Tools area on 
the MouthpieceWork Yahoo site.  There is a file of supplier 
contacts.  Also a comparison of the kits.

Winslow will occassionally deal with customers directly, but they 
have also been difficult at times.  They seem to have an odd 
bisiness "plan".

Many of us have just made up our own kits.  There are sources listed 
for individual items recommended.

We have discussed this in the past but we are getting so many posts 
now, it is difficult to wade through them all and find stuff.  The 
Search feature on the Yahoo site often does not produce info that I 
know has been previously posted.  If you still can not find what you 
are looking for, ask again and we'll help out.

Welcome!
