FROM: esteban_cadenza (Steve Keller)
SUBJECT: Re: revamping the mouthpiece, reed or ligature
The Wanne mouthpieces have an integrated ligature.  Others in the past have done the same thing.  

This doesn't mean you can't take the lig off and use a different one, but the Wanne is made as a system.
