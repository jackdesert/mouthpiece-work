SUBJECT: Re: Laser cut facing curves
Laser Cut Guardalas have very good facing curves, but I can still measure
areas (bumps and flats) that I can correct by hand on most of them.  I do
not know if they are indeed laser cut, but they probably are.  Their box
says they are hand finished but they are not according to Jeff Powell of
Guardala.

A well-implemented radial arc curve is tough to beat on sax.  Flat spots
can add edge to the tone but also add some resistance (poorer response).  A
little more curve near the tip can help altissimo.
