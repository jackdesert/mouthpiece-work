FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul Coats)
SUBJECT: Re: Vandoren long facings
What would really be important is to see the measurement of the .010" 
feeler, the "break" area of the facing.  If two mouthpieces had similar 
measurements for the .010" feeler, but one had the .0015" (which 
normally is used to quote the facing length) at 5 mm longer than .010", 
and the other was 10 mm longer than the .010", the two mouthpieces would 
play very nearly the same.

Paul
