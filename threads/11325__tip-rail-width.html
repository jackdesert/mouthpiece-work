FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: Tip Rail Width ?
You can try and measure the tip rail widths on your favorite mouthpieces and use that.  .022" might be typical.
 
Average the left and right readings.  You can modify a spreadsheet so that you can enter a reading for the left and right rails and let the spreadsheet do the averaging.  If you can not do this, then I do not think you should be using a spreadsheet until you learn some basic editting skills.

FROM: crunchie_nuts (crunchie_nuts)
SUBJECT: Re: Tip Rail Width ?
It depends where you measure the tip opening from.  If you measure it from the very front of the mouthpiece, use zero as your tip rail width.  If you measure the tip opening from the inner edge of the tip rail, then you should also measure the tip rail width and input that number into the spreadsheet.  This is what I do since I find it easier to measure the tip opening at that point.
