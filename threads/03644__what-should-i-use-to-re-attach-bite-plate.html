FROM: tenorman1952 (tenorman@...)
SUBJECT: Re: What should I use to re-attach Bite Plate
Epoxy does not adhere well to either the metal or the plastic.  Use E6000 or Alene's
7800.  These are in the craft department of Walmart, or craft and hobby stores such
as Michael's.  Paul

FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul Coats)
SUBJECT: Re: What should I use to re-attach Bite Plate
Epoxy is brittle, does not glue well to metal long term.

I like E6000, an SBR adhesive (styrene butadyene rubber).  A little will 
ooze out when you put in the bite place, let it.  After it is dry scrape 
off the excess with your fingernail.

You may also use a gel type CA adhesive.

Paul
