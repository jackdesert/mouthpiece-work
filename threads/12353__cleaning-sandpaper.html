FROM: moeaaron (barrylevine)
SUBJECT: Re: Cleaning sandpaper
 

You can rinse off silicon carbide sheets labeled "wet or dry" under
running water while rubbing gently with fingers to unclog. 

Lay them
between a few sheets of newspaper on a flat surface, under something
heavy and flat, so they dry flat. 

Of course, abrasive is gradually
lost with use. 

You need new sheets when the old ones don't cut
adequately.
