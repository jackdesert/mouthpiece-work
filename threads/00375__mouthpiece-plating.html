I've been working on hot roding a Link STM.  I'll post some details

Anyone know about the details of the plating?  The one I'm working on 
is scuffed up in spots.  I can see the base brass, a layer of silver, 
a layer of shiny gold, and a cloudy suface layer that is a mystery to 
me.  I'm not sure if it is another layer of gold or a laquer or 
what.  It could just be crud.  It is thin, but I can feel the 
thickness with my fingernail.

I noticed it when I was trying to remove some surface scratches with 
steel wool.  It seemed like a layer of "skin" was coming off.  I 
stopped because I was not sure if I wanted to strip off the gold.  
Now, I'm thinking of having it replated in silver anyhow so I may dig 
into it again.  I'll probably try lacquer thinner first.

Any ideas?
