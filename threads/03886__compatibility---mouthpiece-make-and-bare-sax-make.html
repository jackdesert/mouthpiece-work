mpc brand is more compatible with certain brand saxophone due to the

(don't know tip opening - charts not clear), Rovner HR Wilkinson SB-9 C.P.

> My question: Are there concerns when I set-up my equip that certain 
brand
> mpc brand is more compatible with certain brand saxophone due to the
> internal bore size dimension relation of the mpc, neck and body of 
the sax?

I find this is not a major concern on larger saxes.  I think it helps 
on soprano sax to be aware of chamber volume effects on intonation.  
But the player's embouchure and concept of sound are generally bigger 
factors in choosing a mouthpiece/reed/sax combo.  

Some vintage bari and tenor saxes are reported to have problems of 
mouthpieces "falling off the cork".  Martin and Conn 12M baris are 
frequently mentioned.  Owners need to find mouthpieces that work or 
they get the neck lengthened by 1/2"

I use a reworked Runyon Metal Quantum 11 and Fibracell reeds on a YBS-
52.  I have a large lower lip I roll in so I tend to play on the dark 
side.  I need some brightness built into my equipment to compensate.
