FROM: danny_troy2000 (danny_troy2000)
SUBJECT: Re: Repairing a crack in a rubber mpc
I would try JB Weld.   It's the first product listed on this link:
http://www.jbweld.net/products.html which is the slow set type.
I would rough up the area that will be covered by JB Weld with fine 
sandpaper to give better adhesion.  Use a light coat so you won't 
change the mpc tone.  I once fixed a Berg HR mpc using JB Weld and it 
lasted for many years, until I sold it.  It's probably still being 
used today.
