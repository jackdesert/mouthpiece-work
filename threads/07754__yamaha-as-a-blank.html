FROM: norman.smale (norman.smale)
SUBJECT: Re: yamaha as a blank
The "standard" Yamaha mouthpieces are made of a hard composite material.
They also make the mouthpiece in ebonite (hard rubber) with the same facings but claimed to be hand finished.  In the UK the ebonite versions are about 2-3 times the price of the standard ones, but still somewhat less than say Vandoren.
