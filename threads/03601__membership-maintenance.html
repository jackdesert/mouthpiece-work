Yahoo as a rule does not consistantly retest bounced addresses.  It is also
VERY sensitive to any bounce.

On the lists I manage two or three retrys are manually sent before any
action is taken.  You do this from the Members/Bouncing/History (each one
has this) link.

A lot of people find dealing with the yahoo account interface troublesome,
so if I know the person and they are bouncing I will drop them a note (I had
been keeping a model message for this to cut & paste, but can I find it
now...?!)  from a non-Yahoo address, as sometimes their ISP simple is
blocking Yahoo (Yahoo is arrogant about meeting the technical requirements
that keep them from being blacklisted by services like SORBS, which is the
reference many ISP's use to bounce likely spam).

Drop me a note off list if I can be of further help!

Steve W
