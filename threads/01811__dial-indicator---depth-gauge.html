>The Winslow gage is a standard dial indicator mounted in a custom made
>metal block.  McMaster-Carr and other industrial supplies companies (MSC,
>Grainger) have dial indicators.  You'll need a friendly machinist to make
>the block for you.  Its probably anodized Aluminum, but I don't know that
>for sure.  Steel would be too heavy and would rust unless plated.
>
>I'm finding a machinist's depth gage with a 6" long base works quite well.
>I got one for $60 off eBay.
