Hi All,

Just a few lines to introduce myself.  My name is Griff and I'm a

mouthpiece design and refacing. refaced a few damaged Dukoffs and the

Hello Pete & Griff,
Pete, your Console is a large bore clarinet from the 50's. 
The Calteau's I used in 1958 were ivory coloured hard plastic costing
about Â£2 sterling then. 
I remember they came in tip openings 1/2/3 and I found them rather
close for Jazz, resulting in my first crude attempts at refacing, on a
smooth doorstep at RAF Nicosia, Cyprus.
With all the info available here, I would not recommend this approach
today.         Eddie.
