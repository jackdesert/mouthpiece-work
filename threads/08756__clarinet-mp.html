FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: Clarinet MP
I think Vandoren measures openings at the very tip and I measure to the inside of the tip rail.  So this can explain .057" vs .054".   Vandoren is probably within +/- .001" of their target.  

Measuring at the very tip is much more difficult to take accurately.  You need a gage with a pointed probe and you must sneak up to the tip without falling off the edge.  It is even more difficult with calipers and not possible with a wand or flat probe machinist depth gage.
