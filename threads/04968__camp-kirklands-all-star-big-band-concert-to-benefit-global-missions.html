Mouthpiece Work / Camp Kirklands All Star Big Band Concert to Benefit Global Missions
FROM: ytrac (Bill Carty)
SUBJECT: Camp Kirklands All Star Big Band Concert to Benefit Global Missions
Join Camp Kirkland and his All Star Christian Big Band on Friday 
night May 18th at 7:30 pm for a benefit concert to raise funds for 
Global Missions Project. GMP is a non for profit organization that 
send music missionaries all over the world spreading the good news of 
Jesus Christ.

The concert will be at Johnson Ferry Baptist Church in Marietta, Ga. 
The church website is www.jfbc.org for directions.

The lineup for this concert is amazing!!  Christian jazz artists from 
all over the world are coming in to play this concert. Artists 
include Lanny Cox {Former Sax player for Truth}, Ron Freed{ The 
Dancing Bear, formerly from Truth}, Bill"BC" Carty {played with Earth 
Wind and Fire, Chicago, and Frank Sinatra}, and the amazing Tami 
Pryce {Christian Recording Artist} just to name a few.

The concert is free to the public, and a love offering will be taken. 
You will also have the chance to pre-order the DVD of this amazing 
night there at the concert. All proceeds from this event go to Global 
Missions Project.

For more information, please call Global Missions Project at 678-766-
5000 or call Bill at 404-992-5780
