the -est of either end of the spectrum, every time I think things

FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul Coats)
SUBJECT: Re: Digest Number 939
Not really.  Paul Cohen has a Conn mouthpiece that he uses with his 
Contrabass Sarrusophone.  And a fellow I am working with on this project 
has an apparently hand made mouthpiece that was made for a friend's cbs 
Sarrusophone.   Runyon Products makes (and has for years) a single reed 
mouthpiece for bassoon (uses standard clarinet reed).  And Jack Linton 
made a tiny silver mouthpiece for oboe.  This is not uncharted territory.

Paul Coats
