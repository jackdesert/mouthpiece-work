SUBJECT: Removable Baffles
Still experimenting with removable, friction fit, polycarbonate baffles……here's todays version…..the goal is 

to be able to easily insert the baffle and get a complete change in tone while retaining good playing 

characteristics…..one thing we know for sure is that TINY changes in the shape of the baffle can make a very 

big difference!
