Welcome aboard, Zach.

One of the first things to look at, in the archives, is a paper by Santy 
Runyon about Clarinet mouthpieces.  This was a series of articles the 
explain how facings, baffles, chambers, etc, all affect tone and 
response.  This applies as well to saxophone.  In case you don't locate 
it, I will email it directly to you.
