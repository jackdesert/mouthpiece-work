FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: who to fix a tip...
To match the creamy white color, consider a local Dentist.  They may be
able to build it up with dental epoxy correctly tinted to match.  Then you
can pick the refacer of your choice.

I have a cream color epoxy given to me by JVW to experiment with.  It is
smelly to work with and he did not care for it.  I've used it once on a
Personaline bite plate and it was OK.  I dont know how it would work out on
a tip corner.
