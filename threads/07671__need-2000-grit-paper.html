FROM: jbtsax (John)
SUBJECT: Re: need 2000 grit paper
Smallparts.com is a convenient source for these types of products.  Under sandpaper you will find the 3M Light Blue Ultrafine 9 micron  paper that is the equivalent of 2000 grit.

If you are polishing a plastic clarinet barrel, you also might consider putting it snugly on the right size dowel, holding the dowel in a vise, and hand "ragging" it with a soft cloth using a fine metal polish. Just don't get too carried away and generate too much heat with the friction! When finished just wash it off and hit it with a coat of Pledge furniture polish and you have a nice finish.
