SUBJECT: Brass Tenor Sax Mouthpiece Blanks For Sale
I have a bunch of mouthpiece blanks I am selling for $18 each. 5 for $80, 10 for $150.  All have high baffles and small to medium throats. Many are similar to a DG King design. But they do vary since these were prototypes made by Carlos Atti of Italy.  They play as-is but need hand finishing and plating.  They can be customized by enlarging the chamber, lowering the baffle and putting your own facing on it.  Some are engraved and most have side flutes on the body.  Email me if interested and I can send you a spreadsheet of what I have.  The photos on my site are a sampling of what is available.
