SUBJECT: OT: Has anybody tried these Korean-made synthetic reeds?
(Not quite OT, since we are always dealing with the mouthpiece-reed 
combination)

Of Korean manufacture, but beyond that, very little info can be found, 
and so far, I have not found any user reviews online.
While I realize that preferences are very individual, comparisons with 
other synthetic reeds might be helpful.

On ebay:  http://tinyurl.com/neuqaq5
On amazon: http://amzn.com/B00CE1GFI4

As can be seen, there are white and black versions.
The white is described as having "clear tone", the black as having "soft 
tone" which (I guess) means a darker sound.

Barry Levine
