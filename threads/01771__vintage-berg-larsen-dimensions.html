FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul Coats)
SUBJECT: Re: Vintage Berg Larsen Dimensions
MOST of the curve would be the same, with only the slightest change on 
the .024", .031" (or .035"), .048" (or .050") and larger feelers.  Only 
about a 1mm change on the .063" and .077" feelers.

Paul Coats
