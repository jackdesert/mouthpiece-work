FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: larger tip openings vs. facing length
I have only seen a few RPC tenors and baris.  They had medium-long facing lengths.  The 38 = 19mm facing length for the RPC alto looks a little short to me for a .090".  
 
There are very few mouthpieces out there with tips that measure over .095".  Alto facing lengths mostly max out at 46 = 23 mm unless there is a problem with them like a convex table.   If they are longer, the reed will not bend and air will leak out the sides.  Some reed cuts might work at 48 and some might leak at 46.
