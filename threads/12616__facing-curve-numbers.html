FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: Facing Curve numbers [1 Attachment]
A curve can be fitted through the data.  Typically, a radial or elliptical curve will not fit 5 feeler data well. 
But these curves will show spots in the data that are high or low.  I think these are almost always defects, not features that should be copied.  

But if you want to copy them, fit the data with a polynomial curve.  Then use the equation of the curve fit wth your set of feelers.  

Send your spreadsheet and I can show you an example by modifying it.
