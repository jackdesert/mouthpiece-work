FROM: kymarto (Toby)
SUBJECT: Re: about side rail thickness (unequal) : does it matter ???
Uneven side rail thickness is not a big deal in any case: it may even be part of the characteristic sound of the mpc. The real important consideration is the evenness of the curve of the rails: where the reed hits, but even there, if the mpc works for you it would be better to leave it alone. If it ain't broke...

Toby
