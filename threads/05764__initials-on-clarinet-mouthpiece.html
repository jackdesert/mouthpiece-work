SUBJECT: Re: Initials on clarinet mouthpiece
I don't think he did the facing himshelf, the link factory did a "Joe  
allard" model, as thex did for coleman hawkins or wolfe tayne.
Joe allard is actually one of the most famous saxophone teacher in  
the jazz world
(people like Coleman Hawkins, Dave Liebmann, etc had lessons with Joe).
you can google a bit and find out about his teaching theories.
I think he's the one to check out for jazz player.

regards
francois
