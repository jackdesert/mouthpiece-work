SUBJECT: Partially complete Saxgourmet Delrin mouthpiece
PARTIALLY complete Saxgourmet Delrin mouthpiece coming out of the CNC machine......we haven't named this model, or decided on the final color.....any suggestions? We believe that Delrin (and there are quite a few varieties) may well be the ultimate material for saxophone mouthpieces…..it machines beautifully (and incredibly precisely) and exhibits truly superior acoustic qualities…..we still have much to learn about this unique and exquisite material, but I wanted you to see that our experiments are not “just talk”……I’ll keep you posted as this develops……when better saxophone products are needed, Saxgourmet will bring them to you…….please feel free to ask questions……I’ll be happy to answer what I can, provided it is not proprietary information
