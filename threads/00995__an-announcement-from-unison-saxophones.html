We have discussed building other members of the saxophone family,
including a bass, a low B flat bari, a C soprano, and a sopranino.
Unison is a small company, and we're currently having trouble meeting
demand for your current line. We don't think that the potential demand
for these horns justifies the tooling costs. We may possible reconsider
this when we are caught up on existing orders, but right now it's just
not an option for us.
