FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul Coats)
SUBJECT: Re: Selmer Soloist G Soprano Mouthpiece
Who knows WHAT the measurements should be from Selmer? 

A good facing length is 18-19 mm.

.0015"    18 mm
.0010      13 mm

Tip opening  D = .051", E = .053", F = .057", G = .061" 

This is what they SHOULD be... they all vary, facing lengths vary.

Paul

FROM: tenorman1952 (Paul Coats)
SUBJECT: Re: Selmer Soloist G Soprano Mouthpiece
I measured a S80 C* just this afternoon... one side rail (back onto the 
table actually) was 23.5 mm with the .0015" feeler, the other side was 
17 mm.  After several sheets of cutting paper, I got the table flat.  I 
measured a S90 180 facing ALTO a few weeks ago, with a 16 mm facing 
length, and a ridiculously small tip opening.

With Selmers, I commonly see warped tables and facings, and out of spec 
tip openings.  No wonder players complain that you have to try more than 
a few to "find a good one".

I do not see why that fine company tolerates that.

Paul
