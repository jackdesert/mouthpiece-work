FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: E-Z LAP Lapping Plate Info
A "real" lapping plate is used by a machine shop to flat finish metal
parts.  A lapping compound (like a gritty mud) is put on it.  Grooves are
cut into the plate so the excess compound and metal dust has a place to go.

The E-Z Lap is not a true lapping plate.  But I think it is better suited
for refacing needs.
