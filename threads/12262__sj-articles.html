SUBJECT: SJ Articles
I just uploaded a bunch of Sax Journal articles relating to mouthpieces and refacing.  I started this 13 years ago with scans to jpg files.  I then let the rest sit until yesterday when I scanned them to pdf files.  Scanning was a lot more involved 13 years ago.
 

 There is a doc file of all the articles one of our group members put together 13 years ago.  I think I am missing a few of the early articles of interest.  There are a few missing from the list too that I uploaded.
 

 Most of the articles are by Ralph Morgan.  I have a love/hate relationship with them.   I loved that at least someone was willing to write about this craft.  But a lot of what he wrote as fact was opinion/misinformation that can cause grief.  He also failed to provide enough detail to be useful in most cases to those trying to learn this craft.
 

 Enjoy.
