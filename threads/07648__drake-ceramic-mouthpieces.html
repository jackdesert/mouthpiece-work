FROM: saxgourmet (STEVE GOODSON)
SUBJECT: Re: Drake Ceramic Mouthpieces
I only played one, which belonged to a buddy of mine. It was pretty bright,
like a Dukoff or Lakey. It had a high baffle with two different break
points, sort of like a Runyon Bionix. The chamber was medium sized. It was
responsive and even in all registers, but too bright for my taste. Very nice
workmanship. I didn't measure it, but seemed to be an average facing length
and medium open tip.
