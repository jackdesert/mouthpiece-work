FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: cnc

There is a mixture of both going on.  New CNC mouthpiece designs may be typically sent out to large machine shops for prototypes and initial production runs.  If a new bisiness takes off, they may invest in their own CNC machine (s).

Early CNC Wannes had indications of some hand finishing on them.  All the current ones I have seen look 100% CNC made.  

Looks like Jody Jazz purcahsed their first CNC machine last year.  Probably to start making DVs in-house.
