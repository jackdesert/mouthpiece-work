SUBJECT: Re: Digital Caliper
The inside caliper jaws should fit to measure clarinet mouthpiece tip openings.  If not, you can file down the caliper jaws on the outside to make then thinner.

If the mouthpiece table is convex, you will not get consistent results with any gauge.
