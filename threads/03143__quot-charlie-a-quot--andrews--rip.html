SUBJECT: "Charlie A" Andrews, RIP
Our longtime friend and inventor of the legendary Gig Dust and Crud Patch 
line of products Charlie Andrews passed away this morning after a long bout 
with Leukemia. Charlie was still playing gigs (although forced to sit on a 
stool) until a couple of weeks ago, and was a legendary figure on the 
Memphis music scene. He is survived by his wife, Linda, and cards and plants

may be sent to:

511 Lena Street
Sardis, Ms 38666-1931

Charlie was an avid sportsman, a retired music teacher, and a great player. 
He was a constant source of inspiration to me and a source of considerable 
comfort during some of my illnesses. He loved plants, catfish, barbeque, and

people. I'll miss him greatly, and his loss will be felt by the entire 
saxophone community.

 
"There's only 12 notes. It's all in how you use them!" Tim Price
