effect tone?  In the last couple of weeks I've done some fine 
finishing on my soprano pieces - no facing changes - just polishing

completely convinced the tone became darker after polishing the 
baffle.

Has anyone tried to create a textured baffle, maybe adding sand of 
some sort to epoxy? I believe that Yamaha or Selmer have a custom 
neck that has a "bumpy" bore to add brightness.
