boptonetom wrote:

"I'm trying to learn this craft by myself, it not easy!  Is there any
literature or videos/dvds available on mpc work? Thanks!"

Somebody please post the link to Jon Van Wie's site for Tom.

I will send him the pdf manuals I have.

Tom, I would also refer you to some of the files in our archives.  There
is a three part article by Santy Runyon from about 1960.  It describes
various tip opening and facing length, facing contours, effects of
baffle and chamber shape, and other things that affect the clarinet
mouthpiece, but these things apply to saxophone, too.

Please explore these archives.

I have a number of big projects out of the way now, and will try to
shoot some video in the next few weeks.
