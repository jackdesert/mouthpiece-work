FROM: mavoss97 (Matthew)
SUBJECT: 13* tip opening
Mornin' all-

I have a client playing on a .165" tip JJ HR* tenor piece.  It is marked 10* but Jody opened this to 13*.  He wants his Link opened to a similar tip.  Just wondering what you might think the best facing length would be for something like this?  I assume a nearly radial curve would be necessary.  This is the most athletic tip I have been asked to do.

Thanks!

Matt
FROM: e_a_falcon (Eric Falcon)
SUBJECT: Re: 13* tip opening
If the facing length is something other than 50 or 52 it wasn't done at Jody's.  I would try and see if 50 to 54 would generate a decent curve for that size.
