FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: Manufacturer Mouthpiece Facing Specs
Ralph Morgan also published these specs in a Sax Journal column several
years ago.  The back issue is probably still available.  I have it
somewhere.

It was not the holy grail of information you think it would be.  It was all
really closed facings from the 40's or so.  I plotted a few of the curves
and several did not look good.  Could be some typos or they were just taken
off a MP selected at random.  I compared a couple of the curves to some
new-looking vintage mouthpiece that I measured and they did not compare
well.

You will do better to copy the facing curve off a mouthpiece that plays
well for you or use a spreadsheet to calculate your own targets.
