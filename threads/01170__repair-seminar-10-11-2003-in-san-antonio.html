Clinician Information
 
 

Dave Ferens

Dave is a 1975 Allied graduate.  He worked 3 years as Forman of the Reed
Department at Allied and is currently Forman at The Music Room located
in Rome, Georgia.  He is a member of NAPBIRT and a Master Clinician.
 

Wayne Tanabe

Wayne founded The Brass Bow in 1983 in Arlington Heights, Illinois and
is widely recognized for his pioneering work in Cryogenics and
Ultra-Sonic cleaning.  He is the moving force behind MDRS: Magnetic Dent
Removal System.  Wayne is a member of NAPBIRT and a Master Clinician.
 
Steve Goodson
Steve began his career in 1974 and is the current owner of
Saxgourmet.com, an online store for vintage saxophones and repair work.
He is the National Sales Manager, Chief Designer, Endorsing Artist
Liaison of Unison Saxophones.
 
Mark Madden
Mark is the Region 5 NAPBIRT Director.  For the past 12 years he has
been Forman for Senseney Music.  He is a member of NAPBIRT and a Master
Clinician.
 
Rex Bullock
Rex has owned and operated two production shops: R.O.C. Inc. and Novus
Musical.  He has also operated a plating company and a buffing wheel and
compound company.  He worked as repair instructor and clinician at the
big box stores, universities, and NAPBIRT.  Rex is currently the owner
of Ed Myers USA.  He is a member of NAPBIRT and a Master Clinician.
