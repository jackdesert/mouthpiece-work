I'm not sure that I understand your question.

Many of us here take measurements of the facing curve using a 
graduated glass gage with feeler gages.  There are pictures of this 
method on the Yahoo Mouthpiece Work site.

This system provides XY coordinates of points along the facing curve 
which can then be plotted in a spreadsheet like MS Excel.  There are 
several sample spreadsheets in the Files area of the Yahoo Mouthpiece 
Work site.
