Hi all,

Ive been reading posts and observing for many months now and have
worked on about 30 mouthpieces to this point.  I noticed that several
"academic" articles and resources have been discussed.  I am working
on a DMA at the University of Illinois and have access to a ton of
literature, articles, books, etc.  If there are any specific texts
anyone would like to observe, I would be more than happy to dig them
up and post them. I have the entire Wyman dissertation in PDF form,
however it is slightly too large for me to post in the files section.
I know many people had ordered it or were planning on buying it.
Dissertations are now digital so there is no charge for these papers
anymore.  If anyone would like a PDF of it, email me or maybe Keith
knows a way one of these larger files could be posted.

Thanks for all the help through these posts,

Dick Kelley
