FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: LOMAX Mouthpiece Measuring Kit
This is the first time I have seen it.  The tip gauge looks nice, but probably inflates the kit price a lot.  The glass gauge is worth ~$20 and the feelers <$10.  The feeler set may be enough for clarinet work but more sizes are needed for open tipped sax mouthpiece curves.
