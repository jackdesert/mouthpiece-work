FROM: kymarto (kymarto123@...)
SUBJECT: Re: Bonjour,
How do you perform this test? Are you rolling the mpc on the glass? You need to confirm that the curve is the same with rail height as a function of distance, and this is really only possible with feeler gauges or a dial depth gauge used on a glass gauge with distance markings.  And these measurements must be very exact. A rounder sound tends to make me think there is a geometrical difference in the baffle area behind the tip. You should also carefully check the volume with water and graduated cylinders.
