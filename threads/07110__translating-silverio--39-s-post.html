SUBJECT: G-Tune PC tuner
Hi folks,

This is a bit of a dicey issue, but here is the deal:

I was looking for a good chromatic tuner for the PC and
ran across an older app called G-Tune. I find it really
great, as it has many features which are useful, including
a spectrograph, an oscilloscope and needle ballistic
settings, which are very valuable for getting the average
tuning of a note. Lots of other good things too. A very
nice piece of work

It was shareware, at a very reasonable price, but it is
now discontinued and one is not able to register it. I
looked around and found a keygen for it which is clean and
works fine. I would be happy to have paid for it, but at
this point I feel that it is not too sinful to use the
keygen, as there is no other way to get it to work.

Is anyone interested in this? I could upload it to a
file-sharing site for download.

OTOH if this is illegal, immoral and/or unethical forget I
ever posted this message...

Toby
