FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: measuring opening
There are several methods for measuring the tip opening shown in the Photos section on the Yahoo MouthpieceWork site.  But we do not have a picture using a wand gage.  I also have pictures on my site using calipers and a machinists' depth gage.

You should be able to use your calipers to check your Morgan wand gage.  Wand gages are often inaccurate.  Either they are made wrong or are worn.

You can check your calipers using a gage block.
