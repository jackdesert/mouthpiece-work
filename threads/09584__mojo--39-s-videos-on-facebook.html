Lots of folks have responded to specific videos, thanking Keith for taking the time to share his expertise, but I just went through the entire Dukoff reface series in one sitting, and all I have to say is - "Wow".  

Folks, you have to understand what it is Keith is providing here.  The six Dukoff videos are a master class on mouthpiece refacing; look at the previous videos and you have a full semester in graduate school.

Other people charge money for this level of information. I understand the market is limited, there is little profit selling stuff to mouthpiece tinkerers, but Keith is freely sharing his considerable experience; it's kind of like we're all his apprentice.  

Thank you Keith, I hope you keep posting videos, though I don't feel right asking for more, because you have already done so much.  You are an amazing human being.

I believe we all owe him a standing ovation for this, in my experience this is unprecedented.  I for one am devouring these videos like a little kid with a million bucks in a candy shop.

-Steve Keller
