FROM: greatstuffmusic (Geoffrey Secomb)
SUBJECT: Re: Eb clarinet mpc dimensions.
Hi. These are the measurements from 2 mouthpieces I have. They are a
Borbeck, and a Vandoren.  Measurements are in the following order:  tenon
ID, tenon OD, tenon depth, body OD.
Borbeck:  14.00mm +/- 0.01, 19.17, 14.90, 25.29
Vandoren:  14.11, 19.17, 14.80, 25.30
