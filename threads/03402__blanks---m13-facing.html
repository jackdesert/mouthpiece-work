I've only done a couple of radial facings on clarinets, and wasn't 
crazy about them.

I use the specs that I submitted here for an M13 facing, though I 
kinda prefer the M13lyre facing.

specs for on the thing, and trim out the baffle bump on it. It's my 
favourite clarinet piece for classical playing now.
