Also it is helpful to specify the glass gauge increments, E. Brand (each increment is 1/2 mm) or mm's.  A facing length on the E. Brand scale might be 43, which is 21.5 mm's.

Same with tip openings.  The E. Brand tip wands were in hundredths of mm's, so that a tip reading of 135 is 1.35 mm or (1.35 mm / 25.3 mm/in = ) .053" (or "53 thousandths")
