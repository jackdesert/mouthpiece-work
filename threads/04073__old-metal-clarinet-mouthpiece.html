FROM: Sk8nSax (Willis)
SUBJECT: Re: Old metal clarinet mouthpiece
Yes, any clarinet mouthpiece should work. However, you need the 
neckpiece that receives a standard mouthpiece. Many times the vintage 
metal clarinets sold in pawn shops and in auctions are missing the 
neckpiece.

Willis
