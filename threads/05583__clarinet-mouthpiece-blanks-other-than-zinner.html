SUBJECT: Re: Clarinet Mouthpiece Blanks Other Than Zinner
Plastic blanks:  Runyon, Bari, Babbitt, Schreiber
Rubber blanks:  Zinner, Babbitt, Bari
  Metal blanks:  Bari, (Babbitt possibly)
Wood blanks:  Zinner, Pomarico
Glass blanks:  Pomarico
  Potential for great blank/mouthpiece:  Ebay...start with good old Selmer or some such and have at it.
