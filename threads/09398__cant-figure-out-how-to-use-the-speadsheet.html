FROM: kwbradbury (MojoBari)
SUBJECT: Re: Cant figure out how to use the speadsheet
Make sure you make use of all the info posted on the Mouthpiece Work Yahoo site.  It is tedious to read through years of messages, but there are many answers in there.  There are a few posts that have step-by-step instructions.  The Files and Links sections have a lot of info too.

If you are not a spreadsheet user, you may need to take some tutorials on how to do basic spreadsheet tasks.  Havin a local friend show you the basics is often better and they may even be able to show you how to use the facing curve spreadsheets even though they know nothing about mouthpiece facing.  If they are good at spreadsheets in general, they will easily be able to determine where the inputs and outputs of the calculations are.

If you have some specific questions, go ahead and ask them.
