SUBJECT: Re: eveness of scale
I have not played with facing curves in an effort to improve mid-
range notes.  If correcting a curve to a more radial arc shape does 
not do it for a client, I will try some baffle changes.  Adding 
baffle near the tip mostly effects high notes.  Going farther away 
from the tip adds brightness to mid-range, then lower notes.  Use 
some temporary putty to see if you can find some spots in the mid-
baffle area to help you.
