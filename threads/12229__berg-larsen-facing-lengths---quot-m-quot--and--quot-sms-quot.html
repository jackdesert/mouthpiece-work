SUBJECT: Re: Berg Larsen facing lengths ("M" and "SMS")
I have done reface work on about 30-40 Berg Larsen, mostly tenors. All (!) had conveks tables and almost impossible to find where the facing was supposed to start. Most were SMS a few M - but I can not see that there is no difference between them.
 But again: The mpc that players sent to a reface is not the good players with flat tables and nice facings :-)
