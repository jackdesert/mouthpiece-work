FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: Guardala MBII Laser Trimmed
Pictures I have seen of the LT MB2 indicate that it is a MB2 in name 
only.  Its baffle and chamber design resembles a Guardala King.  Dave's 
original partner is Jeff Powell.  He is also the co-holder of 2 patents 
with Dave Guardala.  Jeff has posted that he believes these are 
unauthorized editions of Guardalas.  The German merchant claims Dave 
Guardala authorized him to make these.  It is difficult to determine 
the truth but the facts are that these are being sold and they do not 
appear to be copies of handmade MB2s.
