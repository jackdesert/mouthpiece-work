>How does someone learn about and possibly get into the field of 
mouthpeice work?

Use the resources gathered on the Yahoo MouthpieceWork site.  Not just the
forum, but all the files, photos and links.

If you can locate a refacer in your area, see if you can visit them to see
them work and possibly take some lessons.  Some refacers are a little
squirrelly about sharing their knowledge, but it does not hurt to ask.

Measure, measure, measure. Practice, practice, practice.  Try it as a hobby
first.  Very few turn it into their primary career without taking a vow of poverty.
