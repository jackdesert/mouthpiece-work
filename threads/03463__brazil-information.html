FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)

We do not have any CAD drawings posted that would assist you in making 
a mouthpiece.  Some of them members may have some, but I doubt it.

There are many mouthpiece designs, so starting with someone else's CAD 
design seems strange to me.  A more normal approach would be to get a 
mouthpiece you like and try to copy it.  You should create your own 
drawings of this mouthpiece if you need them.  Or take it to a designer 
or shop that can do this for you.
