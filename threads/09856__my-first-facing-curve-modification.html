FROM: kwbradbury (Keith Bradbury)
SUBJECT: Re: My first facing curve modification
Just like there is no best mouthpiece, there is no best curve.  A radial curve 
is a good place to start as you learn facing work.  It may even be a good place 
to stop.

A well made radial curve will beat out a so-so implemented elliptical curve or 
any other curve shape.  The family of elliptical curves are just a way to add 
some "good" resistance.  You can also add resistance with a harder reed, shorter 
facing and/or a more open tip.  There are trade-offs with each approach.
